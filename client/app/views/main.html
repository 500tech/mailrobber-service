Mail Robber

Simple email testing for Rails

Include gem ‘mail_robber’ in your Gemfile.

Register an app on mailrobber.com: rails g mail_robber:install

View emails sent from your app: rake mail_robber:open

You can customize settings in config/initializers/mail_robber.rb: 

    MailRobber.configure do |config|
      config.enabled = true
      config.block_delivery = false
      config.api_key = 'w29m391z295700382fhsi'
    end
